About Me

I want to take this time to tell you a little about myself. Just a little history about me, I was born in Hawaii, raised in California, and moved to Texas in 2012. I graduated from West Hills Community College with three Associate degrees, and when I moved to Texas I started attending University of Texas in Arlington (UTA) with a major in Communication. I left UTA and started my journey in the workforce through different jobs. I gained a lot of experience working with different types of people from notable companies such as Madison Scouts, Amazon, Eddie Bauer, Verizon, and National General Insurance.

In 2019, I got to point in my professional career where I needed to make a change. Because I have always had an interest in technology, I decided to do some research in opportunities in the technical field. I started learning the basics in web development in my spare time late night and on the weekends. In April of 2019, I decided to leave my then-current position as an Insurance Agent and educate myself full-time.

I am currently looking for a wide array of learning and employment opportunities. Anywhere from small freelance, unpaid/paid internships to part-time/full-time employment as a front-end junior developer.
